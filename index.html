Services we Provide

We offer a comprehensive range of accounting services focused on responding to the most current needs of our community. We strives to satisfy our clients' needs by offering competitive prices and proven results.

Accounting and Bookkeeping

    We manage financial statements and reports, producing an accessible analysis of your business assets to always know the current state of your company's financial health.

Bank Reconciliations

    We specializes in bank reconciliations. We'll make sure your company's banking is in order to avoid bounced or delayed check.

Personal and Corporate Taxes

    We specializes in tax preparation and submission for personal and corporate clients. We help you solve bad taxes or any other issues your company may have regarding its financial situation.

Payroll Services

    We also help your small business navigate audits related to payroll, income withholding, and filing the W-2 and W-1099 tax forms for your current employees

State Sale and Reemployment Tax Report

    Our experienced team can assist your business with the timely report of your Florida 7% sales tax declaration and payment and with your reemployment tax payment and reporting.

Business Incorporation

    Our experienced team will help you to incorporate your new business and will offer you the advice to get you started.
